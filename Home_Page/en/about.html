About Us

Kaisen Gyoza Pekin is a Chinese restaurant located in Miyanosaka, Hirakata City, Osaka.
Enjoy our handmade seafood gyoza made with fresh pork and reduced-pesticide vegetables.
Beyond our signature gyoza, we offer a wide variety of delicious a la carte dishes.
