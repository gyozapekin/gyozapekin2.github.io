Our Commitment

At Kaisen Gyoza Pekin, we take pride in crafting every dumpling by hand, every day. Our signature seafood gyoza is made with fresh shrimp, octopus, and scallops. We even make the wrappers from scratch, wrapping each dumpling with care.

Our restaurant has been featured on the popular Japanese TV show "Narumi-Okamura no Sugiru TV". Please come and taste the flavor we are proud of.

Welcome

Kaisen Gyoza Pekin is a local Chinese restaurant in Miyanosaka, Hirakata City, Osaka.
Enjoy our handmade seafood gyoza, prepared with fresh pork and reduced-pesticide vegetables.
Of course, our other dishes are just as delicious.
From crispy fried noodles and sweet-and-sour pork to karaage fried chicken, tempura, and fried rice, we offer a wide variety of authentic Chinese dishes.
Please come with an appetite.
