Privacy Policy

The Restaurant may collect personal information (such as name, email address, phone number, and address) from customers only to the extent necessary, for example through the contact form, reservations, or online shop orders.

We use collected personal information only for the following purposes:

We will not disclose personal information to third parties without the customer's consent, except as required by law. However, we may provide necessary information to delivery companies for the purpose of shipping products.
